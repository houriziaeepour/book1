The Life Network

A satiric Science Fiction (popular science through story telling)

    Is the most technologically advanced civilization in the Galaxy ready to confront us ?

    Are they able to warn us about our dangerous path that may lead to our extermination ?

    Can we understand their message ?

    Are we wise enough to follow their advise and save the planet ?

The fate of humanity depends on the success of training project of two alien students, career of a young scientist, childhood dreams of a city boy, and struggle of a snob astronaut to recover from consequences of his own foolishness.

About the author

HouriZ. (Houri Ziaeepour) is a physicist and has worked in several European and international research institutes. She has a Ph.D. in theoretical physics from Paris 7 University, Paris, France and a BS in electronic engineering from Tehran Polytechnic, Tehran, Iran.

Her research interest is in astro-particle physics, cosmology, and fundamental physics. This book is her first fiction/popular science.
